## The hero subhead (warm, anticipatory, GetCalFresh-tone)

> **You shouldn't have to figure out the wall.
> We do the math, sequence the path, and hand you the plan.**

Three verbs, in this order:

1. **We do the math** — benefits cliff, PVS, commute time, total cost.
2. **Sequence the path** — barrier graph DAG, ordering, week-by-week.
3. **Hand you the plan** — Career Center Ready Package, action checklist.

Never reorder. The math comes first because that's the wall the resident
can't see.

## Tone fingerprint (for AI-generated copy expansions)

- **Editorial gravity** — Linear / Apple / NYT Snow Fall lineage. Confident, spare, never breathless.
- **Anticipatory warmth** — GetCalFresh / Code for America. We've already done the work; you don't have to.
- **Civic dignity** — never patronize the worker. They're an expert in their own life. We're an expert in the system.
- **Tabular numbers** — when we cite a stat, it's specific and tested. "33 barriers. 53 connections." Not "many."
- **Active voice** — "We do the math," not "the math is done."
